Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lian ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you interact with our content on social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from viewers. When you interact with our content on third-party platforms (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), those platforms collect data according to their own privacy policies.

We may receive aggregated, anonymized analytics data from these platforms, including:

  • View counts, watch time, and engagement metrics
  • Geographic and demographic trends (never individual-level)
  • Content performance data

2. How We Use Information

We use aggregated analytics solely to:

  • Improve the quality and relevance of our content
  • Understand what topics resonate with our audience
  • Plan future content themes and formats

3. Third-Party Platforms

Our content is distributed through platforms including TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs how your data is handled:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • YouTube / Google Privacy Policy

4. Cookies

Our content pages (if any) may use standard cookies for analytics. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via DM on our social media channels.
